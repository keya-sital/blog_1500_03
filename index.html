J&J, SciBase team up to build out skin-screening AI to predict eczema in newborns

https://www.fiercebiotech.com/medtech/jj-scibase-team-build-out-skin-screening-ai-predict-eczema-development-babies
Nov 11, 2022 12:40pm

Johnson & Johnson and SciBase are teaming up to use the Nevisense Go system to detect ezcema, a chronic skin disease. Nevisense Go uses AI to examine the child's skin for abnormalities in a non invasive way. In summary, this technology has made early detection easier on youth and has been approved in U.S., Europe and Australia.
